Our Mission

RentOrBuyHouses is designed to empower individuals to make informed financial decisions about their housing options. Whether you're considering renting or buying, our platform provides the tools and information needed to evaluate both choices objectively.

We believe that everyone deserves access to transparent, easy-to-use real estate solutions. Our goal is to bridge the gap between property seekers and property owners, creating a community where everyone can find or list properties with confidence.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is RentOrBuyHouses free to use?

Yes! Browsing listings and using the calculator is completely free. Property owners pay only when they want to create premium listings.

How accurate is the calculator?

Our calculator uses standard financial formulas for mortgage calculations. However, it doesn't include taxes, insurance, or maintenance costs, which can vary by location.

Is my personal information safe?

Yes. We use industry-standard encryption and secure authentication methods to protect your data.

How do I list a property?

Create an account, log in, and click "Create Listing" to add your property. You can edit or delete it anytime.

Can I filter listings by location?

Yes! You can filter by city, property type (rent/buy), price range, and other criteria in our listing catalog.

What should I know about using this calculator?

The calculator provides estimates based on the inputs you provide. Actual mortgage payments may vary based on current market rates and your credit profile.
