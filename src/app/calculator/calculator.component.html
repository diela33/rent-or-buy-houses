💡 Tips for Using the Calculator

  • Monthly Rent: Use your current or expected monthly rental cost

  • Interest Rate: Check current mortgage rates in your area (5-7% typical)
  • Loan Term: 30-year mortgages are most common in Europe
  • Not Included: Property taxes, insurance, maintenance, HOA fees (for buying)
